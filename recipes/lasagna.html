Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 10 lasagna sheets
  • 500g mince beef
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 150g tomato paste
  • 1 cube beef stock
  • 1 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1 celery
  • 1L milk
  • 200g butter
  • 80g flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chilli flakes
  • Parmigiano Reggiano
  • Olive oil

Steps:

Sauce:

  1. Dice the onion, carrot and celery and add to a medium heat pot with olive oil
  2. Once onions have begun to yellow, add the mince beef in chunks, working with a wooden spood to avoid clumping
  3. Once all of the beef is added and has browned, add the minced garlic clove
  4. After 30 seconds, add the can of chopped tomatoes and the tomato paste
  5. Add the beef stock cube after melting in a mug of boiling water
  6. Season to taste, and allow to cook for 1.5 hours

Bechamel:

  1. Add the butter to a pot and stir until melted
  2. Once melted, slowly add the flour while constantly stirring to avoid clumping
  3. Mixture should have a bicuit like texture, once this is reached, stir in the hot milk
  4. once the mixture thickens, add half of the grated Parmigiano

Building the Lasagna

  1. In a oven tray, layer the lasagna adding first the sauce, then bechamel and then a layer of pasta
  2. Continue layering until no sauce remains
  3. Grate the remaining Parmigiano over the lasagna
  4. Place the tray in the oven at 180C for 1 hour

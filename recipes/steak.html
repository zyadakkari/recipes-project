Steak

Ingredients:

  • 400g Sirloin steak
  • 25g butter
  • 2 sprigs thyme
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Generously rub the room temperature meat with salt and pepper
  2. Heat the butter in a cast iron grill on high heat
  3. Add the sprigs of thyme to the melted butter
  4. Add the steak to the grill, cooking for 1.5 minutes each side
  5. Baste the steak with the melted butter infused with thyme
  6. Remove from grill and allow the steak to rest for 5 minutes
  7. To serve, slice the steak into thin slices and season with salt

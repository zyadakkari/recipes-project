Roast Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole baby chicken
  • 500g potatoes
  • 500g carrots
  • 1 broccoli
  • Chilli flakes
  • Rosemary
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps:

  1. Slice up the vegetables to your preffered size and place in a baking tray
  2. Season the vegetables with a generous amount of olive oil and the seasoning mix listed
  3. Place in the oven at 180C for 2 hours
  4. Place the chicken in a separate baking tray and season with olive oil and the spice mix
  5. Place in the oven with the vegetables after they have cooked for 1 hour
  6. Remove both trays at the end of the two hours and allow to rest for 5 minutes before serving

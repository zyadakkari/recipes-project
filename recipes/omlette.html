Omlette

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 10g butter
  • salt
  • black pepper

Steps:

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl and whisk together
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste
  3. Heat the butter in a pan on medium heat
  4. Add the egg mixture slowly adjusting with wooden spoon until cooked
  5. Fold in half and serve with optional garnish of chives or parsley
